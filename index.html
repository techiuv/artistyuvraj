Featured Works

Explore the gallery of artworks, each crafted with precision and a deep sense of creativity

The End

"The End" by Yuvraj is a charcoal artwork depicting a disintegrating hand, symbolizing the cycle of life and death. The realistic detail and stark contrast emphasize the transient nature of existence, inviting reflection on transformation and rebirth.

Growth

The artwork representing a sentiment of growth and success. Fingers are represented as obstacles that a person face in the path of his/her goal. The Tree comes out from the fingers showing courage to face all the obstacles and reach to its goal. Roots also comes out from the fingers representing that no kind of force can stop you to grow.

The Society of Comparison

"The Society of Comparison," delivers a poignant message about the detrimental effects of comparing children to one another. The piece features a balance scale with two bowls, one of which is broken and has fallen to the ground.

Upcoming Projects

Series of Celestial Horizons

"Celestial Horizons" is an evocative series of artworks that capture the ever-changing beauty of the sky. Each piece in this collection explores different times of day, weather conditions, and atmospheric phenomena, from the serene calm of a dawn sky to the dramatic intensity of a stormy evening.

Fallen In Love

"Fallen in Love" is a captivating charcoal artwork inspired by the scientific phenomenon where the human eye's pupil dilates upon seeing someone they truly love. This collection aims to explore and visualize the deep, often unspoken emotions associated with love and affection.

Techno-Dreamscape

"Techno-Dreamscape" is an original charcoal artwork that explores a future where humanity is intricately entwined with technology. Inspired by a dream, these pieces depict surreal landscapes and futuristic motifs, blending organic forms with technological elements. Through the medium of charcoal, each artwork evokes a sense of wonder and speculation about our evolving relationship with technology and its potential impact on society.

In the Memory of Someone

This evocative piece captures the profound and heart-wrenching feeling of losing someone special. Created with the raw and expressive medium of charcoal, the artwork centers on a singular, agonizing eye that reflects deep sorrow and enduring pain. Through its intense, emotional portrayal, the piece serves as a poignant tribute to the memories of those we have loved and lost.
